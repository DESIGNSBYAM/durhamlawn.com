Our Services

Lawn Maintenance

grass, edging concrete, trimming around objects with weedeaters, trash/limb/branch removal and blowing off concrete where needed.

Pruning Shrubs/Trees

Year round, Durham Lawn Care will trim and prune any trees, shrubs, or bushes that you would like us to take care of. We know the best intervals to make them beautiful.

Sod & Turf Installation

Purchasing, transporting, and installing sod/turf is a project that Durham Lawn Care is perfect for. Contact us for details.

Power Washing

Durham Lawn Care provides a pressure washing service to clean
off concrete surfaces in your yard or on the grounds of a commercial business.

Flower Bed Installation & Other Landscaping

We build flower beds, plant flowers, place flower bed

Mulch & Gravel Laying

During the Fall, Durham Lawn Care offers leaf cleanup and mulching. This ensures your yards look tidy and clean during the Autumn season.
